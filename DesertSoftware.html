SUMMARY

I led the design and direction for a website refresh and its front-end coding

Problem Statement

Desert Software started with WordPress to simply display the basic information of their products, which are OHS (Occupational Health & Safety) app kits for paplerless working environment. The task is to convert WordPress into bootstrap framework to display more information with a modern look in a more flexible manner.

They need more than just a visual refresh, it's the greater flexibility for their content, the capacity to broaden their business types, and a website they can invest more time in scaling rather than fixing.

APPROACH

The largest challenge going into this project for our team was details, how to display products in a comprehensive way, more than just simple text and application screenshots, so we had to be incredibly resourceful with where we focused our time.

With that in mind, we agreed the priority was to focus on the app logic and direct experience that the app brings to end users, together with the actual user experience and comments displayed to enhance product persuasion.

Style Boards

As part of a re-design, our team also set up a guidance on font selections and pairings going forward.

Style board explorations played an important role in decision making on the direction that would be rolled out across web for the brand.

Type Refresh

Desert Software were seeking a more contemporary feel from their type. After multiple rounds of changes, we landed on Helvetica and Google Roboto â€” the brightness and elegance of Helvetica contrasting with the open curves of Roboto.

Our Partners

Desert Software intends to connect with their partners more closely and help them promote their brands through the updated website. So our next step is to focus on a new section, "Valued Partners" to show the collaboration and share their updates.

Buy the Logic

A really good product logic must start from the real needs of customers. We analyzed users' needs through repeated communication and specific questions with users, so as to get what users really want to see. Compared with the simple listing of text and application screenshots of the previous version of the website, our team focused on the logical explanation and practical demonstration of the app.

Through the visual app logic diagram and description, combined with the integrated management dashboard system, users can feel the powerful practicability of product functions.

Project Design System

Feeling frustrated with the limited time allocated for this project, we brainstormed ideas including new design elements and enabled some bold color combinations especially in the new section "Web Design". We identified the key areas that could be re-applied to streamline those early steps in setting up styles and also to align design and developer teams in a fast paced environment.

Learnings

1 option is better than many

When you're communicating your process and solutions to a client who isn't always the key decision maker, it's important to keep your points clear and concise enough for easy re-communication. Even though the client may appreciate the extra displayed effort, it can muddy the waters when feedback comes back out.
